Valorant Wiki

The Valorant Wiki can give you information about your favourite agents, weapons, maps and much more.

Commands:

Command What does it do?
V help Get all the commands
V agents Get all the names of the agents
V agent {AGENT_NUMBER} To get more info about a agent
V random Get a random agent to play with
V weapons Get all the names of the weapons
V weapon {WEAPON_NUMBER} Get more info about a weapon
V maps Get all the names of the maps
V map {MAP_NUMBER} Get more info about a map
