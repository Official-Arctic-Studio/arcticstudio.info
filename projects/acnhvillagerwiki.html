ACNH Villager Wiki

Get information about your favourite villagers in Animal Crossing.

Commands:

Command What does it do?
ac help Get the all the commands
ac get NAME Get info about the given villager
ac today Get the villagers that have their birthdays today
ac who (month/day) Get the villager that has their birthday on the given date
ac setup Setup the notifications channel
ac stop Stop the bot from sending the notifications
